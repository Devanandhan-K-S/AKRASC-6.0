About The Conclave

Uniting Kerala's Robotics Community

AKRASC is the flagship annual conclave of the IEEE Robotics and Automation Society — Kerala Chapter. Each year, it brings together students, faculty, industry professionals, and researchers from across Kerala to celebrate innovation and advance the field of robotics and automation.

From hands-on workshops and live robot demonstrations to keynote talks and competitive events, AKRASC 6.0 is the ultimate platform for the next generation of automation engineers.

Robotics Competitions

Showcase autonomous robots in real-world environments.

Tech Workshops

Hands-on sessions on ROS, Computer Vision, and firmware deployment.

Industry Talks

Keynotes from leading automation professionals and global researchers.

Research Expo

Present research and gain feedback from established domain expert bodies.

IEEE RAS KERALA CHAPTER

RAS KC

The IEEE Robotics and Automation Society (RAS) Kerala Chapter is one of the most active and vibrant chapters in the IEEE community, bringing together researchers, professionals, and students who are passionate about robotics, automation, and intelligent systems.

It plays a vital role in promoting robotics education and research in Kerala by connecting academia, industry, and research communities through a wide spectrum of activities.
